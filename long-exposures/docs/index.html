Long Exposures

Turn a Live Photo or video into a long exposure. You pick the frames.

What it does

Long Exposures blends the frames of a Live Photo or video into a single image: silky water, light trails, motion blur, the kind of shot that usually needs a tripod and a DSLR. Unlike the built-in effect, you choose exactly which frames blend, with a frame-by-frame timeline and a live preview. Everything runs on your device.

Common questions

  • Why does it ask for photo access? To import the Live Photo or video you want to blend. It only reads what you pick.
  • Why camera access? Only if you use in-app Capture to record a new clip.
  • Where do my exposures go? To your Photos library (with your permission) and the app's own library on your device.
  • Does anything get uploaded? No. All processing is on-device.
No data collected

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Long Exposures does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All photo and video processing happens entirely on your device. The app accesses your photo library and camera only to import or capture the clips you choose to edit, and only while you are using those features. Nothing you import, capture, or create is uploaded to any server. The app has no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Images you save are written to your own device: to your Photos library (with your permission) and to the app's private library, which is removed if you delete the app.

Because there is no data collection, there is nothing to request, export, or delete from us. If you have any questions about this policy, email james.william.hou@gmail.com.
